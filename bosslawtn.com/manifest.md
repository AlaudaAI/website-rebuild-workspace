## Site Understanding

Boss Law is a family law firm based in Murfreesboro, Tennessee, founded by Mitchell E. Shannon with attorneys Abigail L. Burnham and Giselle Ortega Sutherland (Spanish-speaking). The firm specializes exclusively in family law matters including divorce, child custody, adoption, domestic violence, and related areas throughout Middle Tennessee. They serve clients in Murfreesboro and surrounding areas including Smyrna, La Vergne, and Woodbury, offering both English and Spanish legal services with a focus on personalized client representation.

## Civil Litigation Attorney in Murfreesboro TN

Civil litigation is a process of resolving civil disputes in the courts. Civil matters tend to revolve around relationships between people, such as real estate or contract disputes between businesses. Unlike [criminal cases](https://borotnlaw.com/practice-areas/criminal-law/) that usually involve an individual versus Government, these lawsuits are about individuals suing other individuals or companies for damages caused by them during their professional relationship with each other.

If you live or work in Murfreesboro TN, and believe that someone else has legally wronged you, you may consider filing a lawsuit against them. But civil cases are typically fiercely contested. There's a lot to think about before you file for a lawsuit. The first thing you should do is find an Murfreesboro experienced civil litigation attorney to walk you through the process and give clear advice on how best to proceed with your case!

**At Boro TN Law**, we believe in taking on an individualized approach to each client's needs – after all, this is what matters most. We go above and beyond by providing outstanding legal service not just now but into the future as well through careful planning so that everything runs smoothly.

Our experienced civil attorneys will help you negotiate contract disputes if one party fails to fulfill its obligation according to the terms of the contract. Contract litigation typically involves forming contracts, breach of contract, and implementation of the contract, along with other matters. At Boro TN Law, we help our clients obtain compensation when another party's wrongful conduct (intentionally or through negligence) causes harm. Various instances can lead to a valid **_personal injury claim, including; auto accidents, medical malpractice, or worker's compensation_**.

Real estate disputes involve many factors, including specific performance, boundary and title disputes, real estate fraud, partition, and breach of contract. Real estate lawsuits can be complex to navigate on your own. That's why you need to consult our experienced civil law experts in Murfreesboro for assistance.

Over the years, the civil attorneys at Boro TN Law have represented tenants and landlords involved in legal disputes. This experience allows them to have an insight into the rights of each party during case proceedings, as well as challenges each party faces when looking for solutions.

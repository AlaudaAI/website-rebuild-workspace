## Site Understanding

Zettersten & Hammond PLLC is a family law firm serving Williamson, Maury, and Rutherford Counties and the greater middle Tennessee area, with offices in Brentwood and Murfreesboro. The firm specializes exclusively in family law matters including divorce, child custody, mediation, property division, and alimony. Founded by Christina Hammond Zettersten, the firm takes a compassionate, results-oriented approach and offers both litigation and mediation services to help families navigate sensitive legal issues. The firm emphasizes putting children's best interests first and helping clients achieve positive outcomes through both collaborative and adversarial processes.

## Site Understanding

Turner Law Offices, P.C. is a full-service law firm based in Murfreesboro, Tennessee, led by attorney Rob Turner with over 25 years of trial experience and having handled over 20,000 clients. The firm specializes in family law (divorce, child custody, child support), criminal defense, DUI, bankruptcy, and estate planning. They serve Murfreesboro and surrounding middle Tennessee counties, emphasizing personalized attention with free initial consultations and positioning themselves as experienced trial attorneys who guarantee they will do their best for every client.

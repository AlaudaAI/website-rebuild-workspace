## Site Understanding

Boro TN Law is a general practice law firm in Murfreesboro, Tennessee, led by attorney Micheal A. Jones. The firm specializes in criminal defense, family law, civil litigation, and probate matters, serving clients throughout Rutherford County. Jones brings extensive experience from his background with the Tennessee Bureau of Investigation and Department of Mental Health, and emphasizes providing understandable legal guidance with a focus on results. The firm positions itself as offering aggressive advocacy with personalized attention for families and individuals facing legal challenges.

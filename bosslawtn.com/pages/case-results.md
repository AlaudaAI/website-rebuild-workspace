# Case Results

[Home](https://www.bosslawtn.com/ "Go to Boss Law.") / Case Results

##### Contested Divorce Settlement – Resolved Before Trial

Negotiated a settlement in a highly disputed divorce involving real estate and joint investments. The case was resolved through mediation, saving the client months of litigation and substantial legal fees.

##### Child Support Modification 40% Reduction Approved

After a major income change, we filed for a child support modification and successfully demonstrated hardship, resulting in a 40% payment reduction while maintaining compliance with state guidelines.

##### Emergency Order of Protection Granted Within 24 Hours

Filed and obtained an emergency protective order for a client experiencing domestic threats. Our team coordinated swiftly with local authorities and secured long-term protection through follow-up hearings.

##### High-Asset Divorce – Protected $2.3M in Business Assets

Represented a business owner in a contentious divorce. Through detailed financial discovery and negotiation, we preserved over $2.3 million in separate business holdings and reached a fair spousal support agreement without trial.

##### Complex Custody Win Primary Custody Awarded

Our client, a father of two, faced an uphill battle for custody after relocating for work. We proved consistent involvement and stability, securing primary custody and favorable visitation terms for the other parent.

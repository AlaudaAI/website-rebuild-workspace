## Site Understanding

KRBS Law (Kious, Rodgers, Barger & Sanderson) is a full-service law firm serving Middle Tennessee from their Murfreesboro office. The firm offers comprehensive legal services including business law, estate planning, family law, personal injury, probate, real estate law, and social security disability cases. They also provide mediation services and have an affiliated real estate title company. The team consists of experienced attorneys including founding member John W. Rodgers, members Jay Barger and Loren A. Sanderson, emphasizing strong attorney-client relationships and decades of combined experience in Middle Tennessee's legal landscape.

# MICHEAL A. JONES

### Attorney in Murfreesboro, TN

Micheal A. Jones is a general practice attorney located in Murfreesboro, Tennessee whose areas of practice include: Criminal Law, [General Civil Litigation](https://borotnlaw.com/practice-areas/civil-litigation/) (Landlord/Tenant; Contractual Dispute; Personal Injury…etc.), Family Law, and Real Estate Law. Micheal Jones also provides Rule 31 General Civil and Family Mediation services.

Micheal is an honors graduate of [Middle Tennessee State University](https://www.mtsu.edu/) (MTSU) where he majored in Criminal Justice Administration with a minor in Political Science. Micheal continued his pursuit of the legal profession by attending Nashville School of Law, where he earned his Juris Doctorate degree in 2016.

While attending law school, Micheal worked full time with the **Tennessee Department of Mental Health** and **Substance Abuse Services**. During his tenure with Mental Health, he served in various roles including Executive Assistant to the Commissioner, Assistant Director of Legislation, and Director of Communications.

Prior to beginning private practice in November 2018, Micheal most recently served as the Public Information Officer for the West Tennessee Region of the State for the **Tennessee Bureau of Investigation**. Micheal is a dedicated and passionate legal professional with years of experience in the public sector who aggressively advocates for his clients in and out of the court room.
